## NOTES FOR TRANSLATION

1. **Egyptian Context**: Several location names are Egyptian cities and governorates - ensure these are properly handled
2. **Medical Terminology**: Health-related terms need accurate medical translation
3. **Brand Names**: "Roshetta", "Kamma-Pharma", "MedConnect" should remain as-is or be confirmed with stakeholders
4. **Numbers/Fees**: Shipping fees (50-150), ratings (4.8-5), experience years (10+) are dynamic
5. **Phone Numbers**: Include in translation review but may need localization for Arabic region
6. **Emotional Tone**: Maintain professional healthcare tone in Arabic translation
7. **Placeholders**: Fields marked with \* are required fields - keep this indicator
8. **Form Validation**: Error messages need to be clear and helpful in Arabic
9. **Date/Time**: Format may need adjustment for Arabic locale
10. **RTL Layout**: Remember to implement RTL CSS when translating to Arabic
